## What it does

Paste your job description. Pick your poison. Get roasted by AI.

| Tone | Vibe |
|------|------|
| 😇 **Nice** | Encouraging — we'll find the silver lining |
| 😐 **Honest** | Balanced — the truth, no drama |
| 💀 **Brutal** | No mercy — maximum existential dread |

You get back:

- **Months until replaced** — your personal countdown clock
- **Annual savings** — what your employer saves by replacing you
- **Risk label** — from *Uniquely Human* to *Already Replaced*
- **The roast** — punchy, meme-ready, slightly personal
